Welcome, snails! We are happy you want to join the IDN and are looking forward to achieving great things together. Both individuals and organisations can become members. Below is information regarding group membership, but If you aren’t applying for membership on behalf of a group, you can learn about how to become an individual member. 


Before applying for membership, please make sure you’ve read and agree with the IDN principles and have a basic understanding of the overall IDN structure. Then, please fill in the form below so we can get to know you better and connect you with the rest of the network. 

This form will ask you basic information about your organisation and about which Circle(s) your organisation would like to connect to. Except for information that you would like us to share publicly, your information will be protected and remain confidential.

If you are not interested in becoming a member just yet, feel free to join our community by joining our public online Matrix server.
