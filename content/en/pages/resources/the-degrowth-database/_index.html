In this little digital library, we’ve got info on over 300 groups worldwide doing incredible work in the degrowth & post-growth space. And if you’re into diving deep, we’ve collected almost 1500 academic papers. Don’t worry; we’ve made them easy to sift through with a handy explorer tool that lets you filter by all sorts of things like topic, language (we’ve got 18 of them!), and even publication type.


But it's not all heavy reading! Check out our media section for over 70 pieces of content, including documentaries, blogs, and playlists (podcasts, lectures, talks, music), all aimed at sparking conversation and spreading ideas in languages spanning the globe. Our shelves are also stocked with around 550 books discussing degrowth in nearly 30 languages, alongside 100+ news articles offering fresh perspectives from some of the world's most famous news publishers.

For those looking to learn even more, we've got about 50 educational programs listed, from master's degrees to workshops, and for anyone keen on connecting or keeping the dialogue going, we've highlighted the communication channels of various degrowth communities.

And, of course, it wouldn't be a community without coming together. That’s why we've included details on over 30 conferences and the gatherings of 4 international assemblies where folks share, debate, and dream up the future of degrowth.

This database is very much a group effort, constantly growing and improving with contributions from people like you. Whether you’re here to explore, find ways to get involved, or even add your own discoveries to the mix, we’re glad you stopped by. Dive in, see what you find, and maybe even get inspired to join the conversation. Let’s make this journey together!
