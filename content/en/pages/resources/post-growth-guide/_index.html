The Post Growth Guide (PGG) offers over 50 resources to empower entrepreneurs and organisations to build and run businesses within the ecological limits of our planet. Join their community of over 2,500 members and start a journey towards sustainable business practices that respect our earth's boundaries.

At PGG, they believe in collective wisdom and in sharing practical insights. Every resource is made to inspire and facilitate a transition to post-growth models that prioritise planetary health and human well-being. There is something for an experienced business owner or a new entrepreneur.

Let's grow together, beyond growth.
