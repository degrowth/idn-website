Welcome to the International Degrowth Network (the “Network”).
This privacy policy aims to inform you about how we, the International Degrowth Network, collect, use, and protect your personal data through our website, degrowth. net.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and will comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Data Controller position: 1 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

The International Degrowth Network is the data controller responsible for your personal information collected through degrowth. net.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Information We Collect position: 2 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

We collect personal information provided by you when you fill out our membership form, which may include your name, email address, and other contact details.
Technical data such as cookies, IP address, and browser details when you visit our website.
We do not collect personally identifiable data unless voluntarily provided by you through the membership form.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- How We Use Your Information position: 3 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

To process your membership request and engage you in our network activities.
For internal record keeping and network management.
To communicate with you about our activities, events, and updates related to the IDN.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Cookies and Tracking Technologies position: 4 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Our website does not use cookies to enhance user experience.
It is possible that third-party cookies (if any) are used on our website for embedded services.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Data Sharing and Disclosure position: 5 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

We may share your non-personally identifiable data with service providers for website maintenance and analytics.
Your personally identifiable data gathered in our membership form will never be shared with anyone outside of the Membership and Onboarding Circle.
Your data will not be sold, distributed, or leased to third parties ever.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Data Security position: 6 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-
