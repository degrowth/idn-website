Welcome to the International Degrowth Network (the “Network”).
This privacy policy aims to inform you about how we, the International Degrowth Network, collect, use, and protect your personal data through our website, degrowth. net.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and will comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

The International Degrowth Network is the data controller responsible for your personal information collected through degrowth. net.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: heading title: >- Information We Collect position: 2 rank: 2 - kind: block template: chapter title: >- Information We Collect ranks: self: 3 data: layout: no_background text: >-

We collect personal information provided by you when you fill out our membership form, which may include your name, email address, and other contact details.
Technical data such as cookies, IP address, and browser details when you visit our website.
We do not collect personally identifiable data unless voluntarily provided by you through the membership form.


How We Use Your Information

To process your membership and engage you in our network activities.

For internal record keeping and network management.

To communicate with you about our activities, events, and updates related to degrowth.


Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience.

Detailed information about your choices regarding cookies and how to manage them.

Information on third-party cookies (if any) used on our website for embedded services.


Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with service providers for website maintenance and analytics.

Your data will not be sold, distributed, or leased to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law.


Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.


Your Rights Under GDPR

Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data.

Right to object to processing and right to data portability.

Right to withdraw consent and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.


International Data Transfers

We will not perform any cross-border data transfers with any of your data.


Changes to This Policy

Any changes to our privacy policy will be updated on this page. We encourage you to periodically review this policy.


Contact Information

For any questions or concerns about your privacy and our policy, please contact us at info@degrowth.net
