Welcome, snails! We are happy you want to join the IDN and are looking forward to achieving great things together. Both individuals and organisations can become members. Below is information regarding individual membership, but If you would like to join on behalf of your group, you can learn about how to become a group member.  

There are two ways to join the IDN as an individual member:

  1. Join as an individual member. If you would like to get involved in the IDN directly or don’t belong to a group yet, you may fill out the form for individuals below. 

  2. Belong to an IDN  member groups. Individuals who are already part of an organisation that is a member of the network are considered members of the IDN. If your group hasn’t joined yet, check out the group membership page.  If you don’t belong to a member group already, you can look at this database of hundreds of degrowth groups and contact them directly to see how to get involved with them. Once you belong to one of our member groups you will be connected to the rest of the IDN through the people your group has selected to be its delegates. 

Before applying for membership, please make sure you’ve read and agree with the IDN principles and have a basic understanding of the overall IDN structure. Then, please fill in the form below so we can get to know you better and connect you with the rest of the network. 

This form will ask you basic information about yourself and about which Circle(s) you would like to connect to. Your information will be protected and remain confidential.

If you are not interested in becoming a member just yet, feel free to join our community by joining our public online Matrix server.
