The Gaian Way works to reconnect humans to the living Earth, of which they are part of and utterly dependent on. We do this through global (online) organizing, as well as cultivating local opportunities to connect with nature and other Gaians. Degrowth is a priority to help humans return from overshoot and into balance with the Earth system.
